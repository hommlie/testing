How do I know if my commercial property has a termite infestation?

Signs of a termite infestation may include mud tubes, damaged or hollow-sounding wood, discarded wings, and frass (termite droppings)

What risks do termites pose to my commercial property?

Termites can cause extensive structural damage to buildings and wooden fixtures, potentially compromising the integrity of your commercial property. This damage can lead to costly repairs and pose safety hazards to occupants.

How often should I schedule commercial termite inspections and treatments?

The frequency of termite inspections and treatments depends on factors such as the location of your property, its construction type, and the presence of previous infestations. Generally, it's recommended to have annual inspections and treatments as a preventive measure.

Are commercial termite control methods safe for employees and customers?

Yes, reputable pest control companies prioritize the safety of occupants and the environment when implementing termite control methods. They use approved products and techniques that are safe for humans and pets while effectively targeting termites.

How long does it take to get rid of a termite infestation in my commercial property?

The duration of termite treatment depends on factors such as the extent of the infestation, the type of treatment used, and the size of the property. In many cases, it may take several weeks to months to completely eradicate termites and prevent their return.
